Our Mission at SFS

Uniting in Service

Uniting the SFS Community in Service is important to the Interact club, as the it is one that has great potential to make a large difference. Being the oldest international school in Seoul, the community is one that is both diverse and posseses many connections.

Improving the Environment

Seoul, South Korea is a bustling place that is home to many. It is in the best interest of the SFS Interact club to maintain a clean environment for all to live in. Moreover, the Interact club would like to work towards improving the environmental conditions of the city in taking steps towards making a more green, eco-friendly city.

Reaching Out

While the SFS Interact club has man aspirations and goals that they would like to achieve, it may not be possible without the help of the outside community. Reaching out for help to accomplish these goals is a step and mission that the Interact would like to work on.
