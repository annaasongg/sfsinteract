Our Mission

As a club, we will be fundraising, volunteering and spreading awareness, primarily involving our primary local NGO. Our meetings will involve us planning fundraisers, planning outings (via which we will volunteer), making posters and more. There are various fundraising opportunities in school itself through the year. The revenue from such will go to the Cclim. Our first outing will potentially involve painting the carts. This is yet to be finalized but is just an idea of what we may do. This club will thus be an opportunity for you to partake in service and give back to the community.

Our Partnerships

Cclim aims at improving the lives of elderly people who collect recyclable garbage for a living. These people, of whom there are many, earn only about 3000 Won a day. This NGO had the idea to convert the sides of the carts used into a place where ads may be placed. This caused a surge in the average pay of the people. Fundraising for such a cause is just one example of a project taken up by Interact. 


Rotary supports over 10,000 clubs in countries all over the world. As a Rotary International Club, Interact will effectively reflect its ideals and causes. These stem from the 6 focus areas that are aimed at building international relationships, improving lives and creating a better world. Our vision for Interact at Seoul Foreign School is a close-knit group that is dedicated to helping and furthering the community. Members may volunteer, fundraise and also spread awareness about certain issues affecting the local community.
